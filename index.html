Breakdown of the NY Times Number One Books

This project delves into the fascinating world of reading habits, drawing insights from a rich dataset sourced from Wikipediaâ€™s List of Lists. The focus is on understanding popular reading genres and the gender dynamics among authors, making it useful for anyone interested in literary trends or seeking book recommendations.

The first graph shows the top genre from each year and how many books of that genre were read in that year. The rest of the graphs provide a year-by-year breakdown of each genre read in that respective year and how many books of that year were read as well.

The last two graphs present a view of the gender disparity between the male and female authors in each year and the number of each in order to give a sense of authors dynamic. The following graph presents a breakdown between the male and female authors in their respective genres, again, in order to shed some light into the gender breakdown of each genre.
